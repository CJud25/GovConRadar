# Hand-labeling protocol (private working document ‚Äî never shipped)

The measured-trust rails publish an accuracy number ONLY after hand-labeled
samples cross the pinned floors in `config/measurement.yaml`. This protocol is
the labeling procedure those numbers rest on. Worksheets are plain CSVs under
`data/labels/` ‚Äî fill them in any spreadsheet tool, save as CSV (UTF-8), and the
next rebake ingests them fail-loud (a malformed row stops the bake and names
itself; a blank row is simply "not labeled yet").

## ¬ß1 ‚Äî Link labels (`data/labels/link_labels.csv`)

**The question each row answers:** is this notice the recompete / successor
activity for *substantially this award's work*? "Same office" or "same NAICS"
alone is NOT a correct link ‚Äî the notice must plausibly be the follow-on
solicitation activity (recompete, renewal, or successor requirement) for the
work the expiring award covers.

**An award's own origin paperwork is never a successor:** if the notice is this
award's *own* pre-solicitation, solicitation, award synopsis, or
notice-of-intent-to-sole-source ‚Äî the paper trail that *produced* the award
rather than follow-on activity for its expiring work ‚Äî label it `incorrect`. A
correct link points at the *next* competition for the work, not the one the
award came from (an exact solicitation-number ‚Üî PIID match posted before the
award began is the classic tell).

**Verdicts** (the `label` column ‚Äî leave blank until you decide):
- `correct` ‚Äî the notice is the recompete/successor activity for this award's work.
- `incorrect` ‚Äî it is not (different requirement, different scope, coincidental match).
- `unsure` ‚Äî you genuinely can't tell from public evidence. Unsure is counted and
  disclosed but excluded from the precision numerator AND denominator ‚Äî never guess
  to avoid it; an honest `unsure` is worth more than a coin-flip verdict.

**The evidence walk (per row, ~1 minute):**
1. Open `candidate_source_url` (USAspending) and `notice_source_url` (SAM.gov)
   side by side.
2. Compare scope: award title/description vs. notice title + solicitation text.
3. Compare the buying office (subagency/office) and the solicitation number
   against the award's PIID lineage where visible.
4. Decide; put one enum value in `label`, any reasoning worth keeping in
   `labeler_notes`, and the date in `labeled_date` (YYYY-MM-DD).

**The freeze rule:** `link_confidence` and `link_reason` are FROZEN at sampling
time. If `opportunity_linking.py` thresholds ever change, published per-tier
precision is WITHDRAWN until cases are re-tiered and re-sampled ‚Äî a precision
number must describe the linker that produced it.

**High-tier scarcity (honest gap):** the current bake's bridge holds
High = 8 / Medium = 898 / Low = 3,257 links. The worksheet is therefore ‚âà128
rows (60 Medium + 60 Low + all 8 High), and the High tier can never reach its
‚â•30-label publication floor on this bake ‚Äî its precision stays honestly gated
("not yet measured") until more High links accrue. This is by design; do not
relax the floor.

**Effort:** ‚âà128 cases √ó ~1 min ‚âà 2‚Äì3 evenings (owner's own hours ‚Äî see the
plan's owner roster). The generator is idempotent: re-running
`py scripts/make_label_worksheets.py --links` never touches a filled row and
appends 0 when targets are met.

## ¬ß2 ‚Äî Outcome labels (awardee-blind)

**The question:** for a recompete unit whose clock ran out in FY2023‚ÄìFY2024, *what actually
happened to the work?* The unit is the honest recompete grain (`outcome_labels.grain: vehicle`):
a standalone contract (clock = its own potential end, with all options); a SINGLE-award
task-order sequence rolled up to ONE case per parent vehicle (`VEH-` case ids; clock = the
vehicle's ordering-period end ‚Äî the next order to the same holder is a continuation, not a
recompete); or a MULTIPLE-award vehicle order (GWAC / GSA schedule / BPA), kept at order grain
because its follow-on is competed via fair opportunity. One `outcome_label` per case from the
taxonomy (`src/labels/taxonomy.py::OUTCOME_LABELS`): recompete_unchanged / extension_bridge /
vehicle_migration / consolidation_or_split / ended / sole_source_follow_on / undeterminable /
successor_out_of_scope.

**The two samples, one worksheet** (`data/labels/outcome_labels.csv`, generated by
`py scripts/make_label_worksheets.py --outcomes`):

- **Stratified n‚âà150** ‚Äî cohort = units whose recompete clock (`potential_end_date`; for a
  rolled vehicle, its ordering-period end) falls in 2022-10-01 ‚Ä¶ 2024-09-30 (any
  `candidate_status`; vehicles still open past the window are gated out; the youngest clock
  has ‚âà21 months of successor visibility net of the ~90-day FPDS lag). A rolled vehicle is
  discovered via its in-window cohort orders, then re-clocked to its ordering-period end ‚Äî so a
  single-award vehicle whose orders all ended outside the window is not in scope. Strata: `value_band`
  (3) √ó component (top-5 subagencies by cohort count + OTHER) √ó `vehicle_kind` (2) ‚Äî
  proportional allocation, minimum 1 per non-empty cell, seeded (`sampling_seed`).
- **Top-50 conditional (disclosed)** ‚Äî the 50 highest-scoring cohort units (a rolled vehicle
  carries its best cohort order's `pursuit_score`; ties: `candidate_id`),
  `sample_set="top50"`; overlap with the stratified draw is recorded as
  `sample_set="both"` and labeled ONCE. **Disclosure:** this ranks by the *shipped
  demo-profile score* ‚Äî precision@50 measures the shipped ranking, not any customer's profile.

**The procedure (per case, in order):**

1. **Blind pass.** Adjudicate "was there a follow-on competition or award for substantially
   this work at this office?" from SAM.gov notice archives plus office/PSC/NAICS continuity.
   The worksheet carries **no successor or awardee identity**, by schema. Commit
   `outcome_label`, `label_confidence`, and the evidence URLs **before any unmasking**.
2. **Notice-anchored preferred.** A judgment made from FPDS records alone (no anchoring
   notice) sets `notice_anchored=N`, which **caps `label_confidence` at `medium`** ‚Äî the
   ingest loader enforces this.
3. **Recorded unmasking.** Only after the label is committed may successor awardee
   identities be opened (to fill `incumbent_retained_observed`). Record
   `unmask_performed=Y` + `unmask_date`. Changing `outcome_label` after unmasking requires
   `label_changed_after_unmask=Y` **plus an explanation** in `labeler_notes` ‚Äî a visible
   contamination audit trail, not a prohibition.
4. **Positive evidence only** for `ended` and `consolidation_or_split`. The absence of a
   visible successor is `undeterminable(no_notice_found)`, **never** `ended` ‚Äî most
   order-level activity simply isn't linkable in public data.
5. **`successor_out_of_scope`** for GSA/NITAAC/assisted-acquisition continuations that are
   invisible in an awarding-agency-097 slice ‚Äî the work continued, our window can't see it.
6. **Effort:** ~150‚Äì200 cases √ó 3‚Äì6 min ‚âà 6‚Äì10 evenings (owner's own hours). The generator
   is idempotent: re-running `--outcomes` never touches a filled row and appends 0 when the
   samples already exist.
